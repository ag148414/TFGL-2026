League Rules

Schedule

  • PGA Tour events only — no LIV
  • Season ends at the Tour Championship
  • During major weeks, satellite events are not counted
  • WGC events not included

Roster

  • Teams of 10 players
  • Players stay on your team for the full calendar year
  • No trades, drops, or mid-season roster changes
  • Mid-season joiners only earn points going forward

Points

  • 1 point per $100,000 in tournament winnings
  • Winnings rounded to nearest $10,000 ($5k+ rounds up)

  • $1,234,999 → 12 pts
